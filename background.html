Rumah Aman (Shah Alam)
Background & History of Rumah Aman Children's Home
Background

'Rumah Aman' means 'House of Peace' in the Malay language. Located in Shah Alam, Malaysia, it provides a caring environment for the care and upbringing of boys who are orphans and/or from needy families. Rumah Aman (Shah Alam) is a foundation which is a non-profit and a non-political charity organisation set up to provide assistance and support to orphans and children from the poor and destitute families and, on a case to case basis, for those in need and helpless.

Presently it manages the Rumah Aman Orphanage and Welfare Home which is a registered body with the Welfare Department of Malaysia since March 2006. It operates the orphanage through donations, zakat and sadaqa lillah entrusted by public. The amount of financial contributions and donations can be applicable for tax exemption by the Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia.

Rumah Aman Shah Alam Orphanage and Welfare Home is a non-profit organisation set up to provide assistance and support for orphans and children from the poor.

The foundation is also affiliated with Our Home : Rumah Aman, a charity organisation registered with the Charity Commission for England and Wales in the year 2004. The charity number is 1102689. Rumah Aman is currently have to branches in Shah Alam. One is located at Seksyen 2, Shah Alam and the second branch is located at Saujana Utama, Sungai Buloh. The total orphans at Ruman Aman Seksyen 2 is about 20 childrens (18 girls and 2 boys). While Rumah Aman 2 Saujana Utama have 16 boys. Their average of age is around 5-17 years old.

May Allah ease our efforts to continually care for these children. It is our responsibility together to care for them, for the sake of Allah subhanahuwata'ala mercy. InsyaAllah.

THE ORIGINS OF RUMAH AMAN

Rumah Aman’s story starts with Mr Abdul Rehman Dakri and his wife. They had always had a dream of opening an orphanage after they retired, and they took every step needed towards achieving this wish, finally starting Rumah Aman here in Malaysia. They founded the home in 2005, and started with less than 10 boys. Their first location was in Seksyen 2 Shah Alam.

Later they moved their base of operations to Saujana Utama. The home in Shah Alam Seksyen 2 is a children’s home for 19 girls and 4 preschool boys. The headquarters in Saujana Utama is home to 45 boys, currently between 7 to 16 years of age, and also serves as the children’s school. As of March 2018, Rumah Aman is home to almost 70 children.

Mr and Mrs Dakri have completely dedicated themselves to the well-being of the children and will continue to do so for the foresee-able future.
