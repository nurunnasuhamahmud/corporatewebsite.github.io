What Rumah Aman do?

Rumah Aman strongly believes that children in an orphanage should not be treated any differently from one’s own. They deserve to live in a safe and loving environment with access to food, water, education and support.

Their orphanage doesn’t just help orphaned children but also those in need, on a case by case basis. They make sure that every child in their care gets:

  • Clothing
  • Proper mentorship
  • Academic opportunities & life training skills
  • Proper personal hygiene
  • A loving home
  • Fun
Objective

Providing a caring and loving home for children that are orphaned or from very poor or impoverished families. Rumah Aman provides not only a shelter, but a home and an education support program to monitor the progress of each individual child, in all aspect. Besides, Rumah Aman also provides assistance and support for orphans and children from the poor.

Mission

To care for the orphans and needy only for the pleasure of Allah SWT, by providing them with a peaceful home, uplifting their Islamic characters and developing their competencies for education

Vision

To promote peace and fulfillment in the lives of the children and people who have deprived of a normal living environment due to unfortunate and unforeseen circumstances. We base our vision upon the Quran and the Sunna of Prophet Muhammad s.a.w. (May Allah bless him and grant him peace).

“Those who have iman and do right actions and establish salat and pay zakat, will have their reward with their Lord. They will feel no fear and will know no sorrow.”(Al-Quran, Surah Al-Baqara Verse 277)
